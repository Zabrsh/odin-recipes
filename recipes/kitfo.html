Kitfo

Description

Kitfo is one of a ethiopians favourite food. it is made with meat, butter, and Mitmita(Spicy). Usually it is served with what we call kocho or as alternative injera.

Ingerdients

  • Meat
  • Butter
  • Mitmita
  • Kocho or injera

Steps

  1. First we will chop the meat into tiny smaller pieces
  2. Secondly we put a butter on a dish and make it on oven until it melts down.
  3. We will mix the butter with mitimita.
  4. Then we add the tiny meat onto the butter mitimita mix, and mix it up.
  5. Finally we serve it with kocho or injera.
