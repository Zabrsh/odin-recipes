Chechebsa

Description

Chechebsa is one of a ethiopians favourite food.

Ingerdients

  • Teff or barley
  • Butter
  • Mitmita or Berbere

Steps

  1. First we will make bread like a pizza with a dough of teff or barley.
  2. Secondly we chop of the bread into smaller pieces
  3. We will mix the butter with mitimita or berbere.
  4. Then we add the tiny bread onto the butter mitimita mix, and mix it up.
  5. Finally we serve it with honey or yoghurt.
