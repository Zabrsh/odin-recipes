Doro wet

Description

There is a reason why Doro Wat is the national dish of Ethiopia and one of the most famous of all African dishes – it’s fabulous! This authentic Doro Wat recipe captures the very best of Ethiopian cooking!

Ingerdients

  • Chicken
  • Onions
  • Olive Oil
  • Garlic
  • Egg
  • Butter
  • Berbere
  • Injera

Steps

  1. First Onionsare chopped into tiny pieces.
  2. Secondly we put the onion on a dish and make it fried with oil.
  3. We will mix the the fried onion with berbere.
  4. Then after the berbere and onion are mixed we add necessary speices on it.
  5. Then we add the Chicken
  6. After the chicken is cooked we will add boiled eggs on it.
  7. Finally we serve it with kocho or injera.
